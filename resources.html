Examples of research fellowships

Including ones our team members have been awarded (✔). If you have additions, please email Jenny.

  • Comprehensive, monthly-updated databases from Johns Hopkins: Graduate, Postdoc, and Early Career Faculty
  • A curated list for minority postdoc funding opportunities
  • Human Frontiers Postdoctoral Fellowships — limited restrictions on nationality
  • Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) — any nationality, to work in an Australian lab
  • LSRF — any nationality eligible to work in a US lab ✔
  • DFG fellowships — for German citizens, or those with extensive scientific training in Germany ✔
  • Swedish International Postdoc — for those awarded a PhD by a Swedish institution who want to research abroad
  • VILLUM Foundation — for Danish citizens to do a postdoc overseas ✔
  • Mobilex grants — for Danish citizens
  • L'Oréal USA for Women in Science
  • Fulbright postgraduate scholarships — for UK or Australian citizens to research in the US

Science skills

How to write papers, peer review, and more — see also our ethics page.

  • How to keep a lab notebook — written for paper notebooks, but most advice applies to our electronic ones. Plus tips for OneNote (LBNL) and LabArchives (Adelaide)
  • A Nature masterclass on peer review, and a peer review process guide
  • A Nature collection on Statistics for Biologists
  • Novelist Cormac McCarthy on how to write a scientific paper
  • Colour-blindness and figures, and a simulator to check how your figures look
  • Finding non-copyrighted images for presentations. Jenny also recommends BioRender and Inkscape/Adobe Illustrator for figures
  • Free copy of Strunk & White's "The Elements of Style" via gutenberg.org
  • The Addgene Blog — e.g. "how to prepare for an industry interview" and "free molecular biology tools"
