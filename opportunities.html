In Adelaide

We currently have no open funded postdoc or technician positions. We are also not accepting new PhD students in 2026, as we're full!

If you are interested in applying to bring your own fellowship to the lab, please email Jenny your CV and a short cover letter explaining why you're interested in our research, and what questions you'd like to tackle.

We welcome enquiries from potential Honours and MPhil/MRes students, and can also advise on available stipends, particularly if you are a current AU student. Please contact Jenny to discuss project topics. Include your CV, and 1–2 paragraphs outlining your research interests and background. Do try to avoid using generative AI to write these — it makes them very generic, and it's very obvious to read.

There are paid research opportunities for undergraduate researchers, for example through the Waite Research Institute (WRI) Summer Scholars program. Information on these and other research scholarships can be found via the university portal.

We also welcome undergraduate students who want to undertake research projects for course credit.
